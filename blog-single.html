Construct EVA communities and win big prize!

June 4th, 2018 •

Recently we've reveived plenty of emails from our followers interested in EVA.The Evangel Team thanks for the attention and involvement of everyone.

Particularly, many people show interests in EVA community.Currently we still have many tasks pending so we decide to hand out the EVA comminity to every follower, after thinking it over.

The community operation model is similiar to EVA node operation model, we will nominate 2 QQ groups and a telegraph group as official groups and others as backup groups, we will issue 100,000 EVA to every official group owner as operation funds. EVA team will evaluate and select official groups every month to keep community active and we will consider following factors:

1.The group size.
2.Activeness.
3.The maintainance and management of community group.
4.the support to official activities.

Meanwhile, we have a big reward plan for the first-period community construction as follows:

3.The cut-off date of the Rewards Plan is 2018/6/31, we'll award the top two QQ groups and top two telegraph groups with the max amount, other groups still have the opportunity to participate in activities later.

4.We aim to create a community to involve EVA followers and make the communication easier, The Evangel Team will be exonerative if anything violating the law happens.

5.July 1st~July 3rd, for all the group owners, please send all relevant data to our official email: EVAprogram@Hotmail.com including:a.group number;b.contact way of group owner(for telegram group).c.group snapshot. 7/4~7/10, EVA will start verifying anonymously, we'll reward qualified groups and make them official groups, the contact will be put on the website.

6.We'll cancel all the rewards once we find cheating behavior and lock the EVA account, meantime the group on our blacklist will never get the chance to be EVA official group.
